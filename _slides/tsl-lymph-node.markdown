    - Capsule
    hint_text: A dense connective tissue covering
  - label_text: "**C)**"
    answers:
    - Paracortex

  - label_text: "**D)**"
    answers:
    - Medulla
    hint_text: The deepest region of lymph nodes consisting of sinuses and cords packed
      with lymphocytes, plasma cells, and macrophages
